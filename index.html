Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2024

Introduction

ChaChaVPN ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our VPN application.

About Our VPN Service

ChaChaVPN is a Virtual Private Network (VPN) application that:

  • Encrypts your internet traffic using the WireGuard protocol
  • Routes your connection through secure VPN servers
  • Protects your privacy on public Wi-Fi networks
  • Uses AmneziaWG technology for enhanced security

Information We Do NOT Collect

We operate under a strict no-logs policy. We do NOT collect, store, or share:

  • Your browsing history or internet activity
  • Your original IP address
  • DNS queries
  • Connection timestamps
  • Session duration
  • Bandwidth usage per user
  • Any data transmitted through the VPN tunnel

Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to us:

  • VPN server configurations you add manually
  • App preferences and settings
  • Language preferences
  • Connection statistics (displayed in-app only)

VPN Connection Data

When you connect to a VPN server:

  • Your device establishes an encrypted tunnel to the server
  • All internet traffic is routed through this encrypted tunnel
  • Your real IP address is hidden from websites you visit
  • We cannot see, log, or store any of your internet activity

Third-Party Services

ChaChaVPN does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Security

All VPN connections are encrypted using the WireGuard protocol with state-of-the-art cryptography including:

  • Curve25519 for key exchange
  • ChaCha20 for symmetric encryption
  • Poly1305 for data authentication
  • BLAKE2s for hashing

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, modify, or delete. All your data remains on your device under your control.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us
